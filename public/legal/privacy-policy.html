Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Arc ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our hub1 smart assistant and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Contact information (email address) when you reach out for support
  • Configuration data you choose to sync with our services
  • Feedback and correspondence you send to us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use hub1, the device operates locally on your network. We do not collect:

  • Voice recordings or transcripts
  • Personal conversations or commands
  • Data from connected smart home devices

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Send you technical notices and updates
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage

4. Data Storage and Security

hub1 is designed with privacy as a core principle. All voice processing and smart home commands are processed locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

5. Open Source Transparency

hub1 is open-source software. You can review our source code at any time on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

6. Third-Party Services

hub1 may integrate with third-party services at your discretion. These integrations are optional, and any data shared with third parties is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your data
  • The right to data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
